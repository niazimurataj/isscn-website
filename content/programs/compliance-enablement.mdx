# Pro Bono Compliance Enablement

We provide free compliance consulting to help businesses establish robust security and traceability programs, regardless of their size or resources.

## Our Approach

### AI-Assisted Workflow

Our technology-enabled consulting approach allows us to serve more businesses effectively:

1. **Risk Assessment**: Complete our guided questionnaire to identify your specific vulnerabilities
2. **Action Plan Generation**: Receive a customized security improvement plan based on your situation
3. **Expert Consultation**: Schedule calls with our team to discuss implementation
4. **Ongoing Support**: Access resources and guidance as you implement changes

### What We Cover

Our compliance enablement services address:

- **Physical Security**: Facility access controls, surveillance, seal management
- **Personnel Security**: Background checks, training, reporting mechanisms
- **Process Controls**: Documentation, chain of custody, inspection protocols
- **Technology Integration**: Tracking systems, sensors, data management

### Certification Preparation

We help businesses prepare for industry certifications:

- **CTPAT** (Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism)
- **BASC** (Business Alliance for Secure Commerce)
- **AEO** (Authorized Economic Operator)

While we don't act as a certifying body, we help you understand requirements and prepare documentation.

## Who We Serve

Our services are available to:

- Exporters in high-risk industries
- Logistics and transportation companies
- Warehousing and distribution operations
- Any business seeking to strengthen supply chain security

Priority is given to small and medium enterprises with limited resources for security consulting.
